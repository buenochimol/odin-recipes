Pizza!

What is a pizza?

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a round dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese (usually mozzarella), and various toppings such as meats, vegetables, and herbs. It’s baked in a hot oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Pizza can be customized with endless variations and is enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients:

  • For the Pizza Dough:
    • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 packet active dry yeast (2 ¼ tsp)
    • 1 cup warm water
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp salt
  • For the Toppings:
    • 1 cup pizza sauce (or marinara sauce)
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Your choice of toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, peppers, etc.)
    • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
    • Olive oil (for drizzling)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a bowl, combine warm water and sugar. Sprinkle yeast over the water and let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
    • Add flour, olive oil, and salt to the yeast mixture. Stir until a dough forms.
    • Knead the dough for 5–7 minutes on a floured surface until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover with a clean towel, and let it rise for 1–1.5 hours, until doubled in size.
  2. Preheat the Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, preheat it as well.
  3. Shape the Dough:
    • Punch down the dough and divide it into two portions if making two pizzas.
    • Roll out each portion into a thin circle on a floured surface.
  4. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Transfer the rolled dough onto a baking sheet or pizza stone.
    • Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough.
    • Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
    • Add your desired toppings.
    • Drizzle a little olive oil over the top for extra flavor.
  5. Bake the Pizza:
    • Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10–12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and browned.
  6. Serve:
    • Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
